General Information

Locations

River River

River River is the longest river that we currently are able to offer floats on. This river passes many great sites and is surrounded by lots of LUSH trees.

Canyon Pass

Canyon pass is known for it's breathtaking scenery and rocky landscape. It is great for those who are looking for a simple ride down the river.

Little Colorado

The clear water of the Little Colorado river attracts all to it. The perfect temperature for a swim!

FAQ

Questions:

Answers:

What are the activities that are offered?

You are able to rent canoes, kayaks, and other floating devices. At the end of the river you will return the rented item.

What are the rates for the bookings?

If you rent a canoe or kayak there will be a 100 dollar deposit plus a 15 dollar rent fee. Other floating devices such as tubes have a 50 dollar deposit and 10 dollar rent fee for the day.

Can I reserve bookings?

Yes, you can. To reserve bookings go to our main site and find the "Book Now" button.
